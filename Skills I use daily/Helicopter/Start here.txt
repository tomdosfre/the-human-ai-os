HELICOPTER: a second opinion for your AI. Set up in about 1 minute.

Built for Claude.

WHAT IT DOES:
Helicopter is the colleague who leans back, sees the whole board, and tells you
the truth. When you (or your AI) start overbuilding, overthinking, or heading the
wrong way, it steps in with a clear call, not a wishy-washy "it depends." You get
the recommendation, the one trap to watch, and the first step, which it takes for
you. For big, hard-to-reverse decisions it can convene a panel of advisors who are
paid to disagree, then a chair who makes the call.

HOW TO USE:
  1. Drop "Helicopter.skill" into Claude
     (double-click it, or drag it into a chat, and click Save).
  2. In any chat, when you feel yourself overcomplicating something, type:
        step back      (or)      am I overcomplicating this?      (or)      helicopter view
  3. It gives you the call. If the decision is big, ask it to "run the panel."

It also fires on its own: while your AI is building with you, Helicopter speaks up
the moment it smells scope creep, before you sink the hours.

No install tools, no account, nothing leaves your chat.
